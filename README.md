# Ω7 — Omega7 Capital

**International Quantitative Research Collective**

---

Omega7 Capital is an independent, student-led quantitative research organization founded by **Adnane Garab** (Sorbonne University, L2 Mathematics).

The collective brings together students from top universities across Europe and North America to produce rigorous, open-source research at the intersection of mathematics, financial theory, and computational science.

---

## Mission

We operate at the frontier of applied mathematics and quantitative finance — not as a club, but as a research collective with real deliverables: papers, models, and open-source libraries.

> *Rigorous Science. Open Access.*

---

## Research Focus

- **Stochastic Calculus & Derivatives Pricing** — Black-Scholes, local volatility, jump-diffusion models
- **Portfolio Optimization** — Mean-variance, convex optimization, factor models
- **Statistical Arbitrage** — Time series analysis, cointegration, pairs trading
- **Computational Finance** — Monte Carlo simulation, finite difference methods (PDE solvers)
- **Machine Learning in Finance** — Regime detection, feature engineering, alpha research
